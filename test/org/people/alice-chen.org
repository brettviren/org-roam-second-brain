#+title: Alice Chen
#+filetags: :person:

Alice Chen is a machine learning researcher focused on natural language
processing and knowledge representation systems. She has been working on
embedding-based retrieval systems for three years, with particular interest
in sparse versus dense retrieval trade-offs.

* Notes

Alice introduced me to the idea of using cosine similarity for document
retrieval rather than exact keyword matching. She recommended the
nomic-embed model family for its balance of quality and local inference
speed. Her thesis work on hierarchical document chunking has been a
significant influence on the chunking design in this project.
